Data retention

We retain personal information until we determine that it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected or retained, or if we are no longer legally required to keep it. We retain some data for the duration of your FightersTech account. This includes information about how you use FightersTech, which helps us understand how users interact with our services, what features or products they may want, and how to improve our services. This includes information such as your username, email address, and phone number. Some of this information may be retained for longer periods for specific purposes, as described below. You can delete your account at any time; additionally, if your account remains inactive for two years, we may delete it.

Retention period for specific purposes

Certain business and legal requirements may mean that we need to retain information for specific periods of time, some of which may extend beyond the deletion of your account. For each type of information, we set these data retention periods based on the reason it was collected:

  • Age verification. If you send us an identification document to request age verification for your account, we will delete it within 60 days after the age verification request is closed. 

  • Backups. Our database retains stored backups for 30–45 days, after which they are deleted. 

  • Compliance with our legal obligations. We may need to retain certain information for longer periods than those described, for legal reasons. For example, when you make a purchase on FightersTech, we are required to retain transaction information for specific periods in accordance with accounting and tax regulations. 

  • Maintaining our security commitments. We retain certain information—including email addresses and phone numbers—for 48 hours after deletion for trust and safety reasons. If a user has been reported or flagged for acting against the Terms and Conditions or Community Guidelines, we may retain that information for up to 2 years. This helps us keep FightersTech safer, prevent fraud, and stop bad actors from creating new accounts or continuing to violate our policies in other ways. 

  • Exercise or defense of legal claims. When you contact FightersTech directly—for example, through support inquiries or requests to access your information—we retain the related information for 5 years from the closure of your request, in case a dispute arises or it becomes necessary to defend our rights. 

  • Service continuity: If you delete your account, we will continue to retain and display content you shared with other users, although it will no longer be linked to your account. This is to ensure that others can maintain access to content that was shared with them at some point. If you wish to delete specific content, please do so before deleting your account. You can do this by following the instructions in the “Information you can delete directly from the services” section, or contact us at privacy@fighterstech.com  if you need assistance. 

When you delete an account, the deletion is executed within a few hours—typically between 1 and 48 hours if everything goes smoothly. Once that period ends, we begin the process of removing identifying data and anonymizing or aggregating the remaining information. This happens quickly—unless the information is subject to a longer retention period as described above—but it may take up to 45 days to remove identifying data from backups. We will retain aggregated and anonymized information, which no longer identifies you and is no longer linked to you as an individual.

As stated in our Privacy Policy, all our users have control over their information and can directly edit or delete their account data, as well as limit the information we process.
