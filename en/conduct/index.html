Code of Conduct and Community Guidelines

 

We created FightersTech with the goal of making it the best place to talk, hang out, and have fun with people online. Our Code of Conduct ensures that everyone can express themselves and find their community—but never at the expense of others.

These guidelines explain what is not allowed on FightersTech. Everyone on FightersTech must follow these rules, which apply to all parts of our platform, including your content, behavior, servers, and applications. If you want to learn about the requirements for developing a bot or other application, refer to the FightersTech Developer Terms of Service and Developer Policy.

If you want to monetize your content, please refer to the additional rules described in the FightersTech Monetization Policy and Monetization Terms.

1. Do not promote, coordinate, or participate in harassment.  We do not tolerate any form of harassment, including sustained bullying, sexual harassment, ban or suspension evasion, and coordinated server raids intended to harass members.

2. Do not threaten to harm individuals or groups of people. This includes both direct and indirect threats, as well as insinuations.

3. Do not share or threaten to share someone’s personally identifiable information without their consent.  This includes offering services that enable doxing, such as buying or selling leaked information or aggregating leaked data from others.

4. Do not promote hate or engage in conduct that fosters hatred. This includes using hate symbols, imagery, and statements that deny the history of human atrocities.

We consider hate speech to be any form of expression that serves to attack others or promotes hatred or violence against them based on characteristics that are protected.

5. Do not organize, promote, or support violent extremism. This includes coordinating violent acts and glorifying or promoting violence or those who have carried out acts of violence, as well as promoting conspiracy theories that could incite violence against others.

6. Do not request, share, or attempt to distribute content that depicts, promotes, or seeks to normalize child sexual abuse. Do not publish or engage with content that sexualizes children. This includes real and manipulated media, animated media (such as lolicon), and any other form of digital media creation (including AI-generated media).

We report child sexual abuse material and grooming to the appropriate authorities in each country.

7. If you are under 18, do not engage in sexual behavior or any other behavior that could endanger your physical or online safety. This includes sexual interactions between teenagers, even if consensual, as well as encouraging or coordinating potentially dangerous behavior, such as vigilantism.

We want teenagers to be able to express themselves as much as possible on FightersTech, but due to the risks involved in online dating, we will remove spaces that promote or facilitate dating between teenagers.

8. Do not request sexual content or engage in sexual interactions with anyone under the age of 18. We report child sexual abuse material and grooming to the authorities.

9. Do not distribute sexually explicit content to individuals under 18. To interact with adult content on FightersTech, you must be over 18 years old.

Server owners must apply an age-restriction label to channels where sexually explicit content is shared.

Users are not allowed to post sexually explicit content in spaces that cannot be age-restricted, such as avatars, bios and custom statuses, server banners and icons, invite images, emojis, and stickers.

10. Do not share, distribute, or create sexually explicit or sexual content about other people without their consent. This includes the non-consensual distribution of intimate media, whether or not it was created with the affected person’s consent.

11. Do not share content that glorifies, promotes, or normalizes suicide or other forms of self-harm. This includes content that encourages others to cut or burn themselves, to starve themselves, or that normalizes eating disorders such as anorexia or bulimia. Acts of self-harm and threats used as a form of coercion or emotional manipulation are also prohibited.

12. Do not share real media that depicts graphic violence, excessive gore, or animal abuse—especially with the intent to harass or shock others.

13. Do not send unsolicited mass messages (or spam) or facilitate such activity through the sale of spambots, server raiding tools, account creation tools, token generators, CAPTCHA-solving services, or similar tools.

14. Do not use self-bots or user bots. Every account must be associated with a human, not a bot.

15. Do not engage with our service dishonestly. This includes artificially inflating server memberships, manipulating engagement statistics, or selling artificial interaction services.

16. Do not buy or sell FightersTech assets, including accounts, usernames, servers, server permissions, and custom server invite links.

Abuse of FightersTech’s paid features and monetization opportunities is subject to the Paid Services Terms.

17. Do not share false or misleading information (also known as “disinformation”). Sharing false, deceptive, or potentially harmful content is not allowed on FightersTech. We may remove certain content if we have reasonable grounds to believe its spread could cause harm to individuals or physical infrastructure, obstruct participation in civic processes, or endanger public health.

18. Do not misrepresent your identity on FightersTech in a deceptive or harmful way. This includes creating fake profiles or attempting to impersonate individuals, groups, or organizations.

19. Do not evade enforcement actions by FightersTech. This includes creating new or existing communities or accounts after being removed for violating platform rules.

20. Do not engage in activities that could harm or compromise the security of accounts, networks, or systems. This includes using deceptive tactics to obtain personal data (phishing), using malicious software (malware), or overwhelming services with traffic to render them unusable (denial-of-service attacks).

21. Do not use or attempt to use FightersTech to promote, coordinate, or carry out financial scams. A financial scam is any deliberately deceptive act intended to obtain illegal, unethical, or dishonest income.

22. Do not engage in activities that generate profits fraudulently at the expense of others. This includes actions aimed at participating in, facilitating, or instructing others to commit fraud. We do not allow coordinated actions to defraud companies, manipulate prices, forge or launder money, or the use of tools that enable illegal activities.

23. Do not submit false, misleading, or abusive reports or requests to FightersTech’s support teams. A report or request is considered false or misleading if it contains demonstrably false information intended to manipulate the teams into taking a specific action.

24. Do not share content that infringes on someone else’s intellectual property or any other rights. This includes sharing or selling cheats or hacks for games.

25. Do not organize, promote, or participate in the sale or facilitation of the sale of regulated or potentially dangerous goods. There are laws governing regulated goods that restrict their purchase, sale, exchange, and possession. Dangerous goods pose a reasonable potential for real-world harm and can jeopardize people’s physical safety.

26. Do not coordinate or participate in illegal gambling. Users are responsible for complying with all applicable gambling laws and regulations.

27. Do not organize, promote, or participate in any form of illegal activity, such as human trafficking or sexual solicitation.

If you come across any content or activity that violates these Guidelines, our Terms and Conditions, or any of our policies, report it by sending a message to support@fighterstech.com . However, please note that we strongly discourage vigilantism, and we may take action against such behavior, as it has the potential to harm individuals and interfere with our investigations and our ability to refer reports to law enforcement.

These Guidelines will evolve over time, which means we may take action against accounts, servers, or content that violate the spirit of these Guidelines if we encounter a new threat or risk not explicitly covered in the current version.

We will always do our best to inform you whenever we update these Guidelines, but it is your responsibility to act in accordance with their spirit.
