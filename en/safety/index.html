Safety

At FightersTech, we take the safety of our community very seriously. Here, you will find how to report inappropriate behavior, suspicious content, or safety-related issues.

How to report in FightersTech

You can report users or issues directly from the app. Here is how you can do it:

If you want to report a user who is violating the rules, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the profile of the user you want to report and tap the "More options" button.
  2. Select "Report user" and choose a category (e.g., "Content related to exploitation or child abuse," "Spam," etc.).
  3. Optional: Fill out the text box to provide more details about the report.

If you encounter issues with a tournament organizer, you can report them directly from the tournament notification:

  1. Open the tournament notification and tap the "Exclamation (!)" icon.
  2. Select the most appropriate category and provide details if necessary.

If you need further assistance or want to report a more urgent issue, please email us at safety@fighterstech.com.
