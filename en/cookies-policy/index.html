COOKIES POLICY

We may receive information from cookies (small text files placed on your computer or device and similar technologies). We and our third-party service providers place our own cookies that allow you to use the services and help us analyze and improve your experience and the services. You can control cookies as described in the “How you can control your privacy” section of the FightersTech Privacy Policy. The services use the following types of cookies:

  • ➡Strictly necessary cookies: These are necessary for our services to function and for us to meet our legal obligations. If you use tools to disable them, the services may not work properly.

  • ➡Functional cookies: These cookies help us provide enhanced features in the services, such as remembering language preferences. Disabling them may affect some service features.

  • ➡Performance cookies: These cookies allow us or our external analytics providers to understand how you and others use and engage with the services, in order to improve them.

Manage Cookies

To control how cookie information is collected and used in the services, you can take one or more of the following steps:

  • ➡You can disable and manage some cookies through your browser settings. You will need to manage your settings for each browser you use. You can find instructions on how to manage cookies in the most commonly used browsers such as Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, Safari (iOS), Safari (Mac) and Opera.

  • ➡To disable analytics cookies, you can use the browser controls mentioned above or, in the case of certain providers, you may use specific mechanisms for that purpose, such as Google Analytics Opt-Out.

  • ➡Additionally, your mobile device may include browser settings to control the use of cookies and the collection and use of data by applications.

  • ➡Third-party groups also allow you to limit how cookies are used for advertising purposes by participating third parties. You can find more information at the Network Advertising Initiative, the Digital Advertising Alliance and, for users in the EU, the European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance.

  • ➡Depending on the location from which you access our services, you may be able to adjust your cookie preferences at any time through the app by going to Settings → About → Legal → Cookies Policy.

If you disable or delete cookies, some parts of the services may not function properly. Information may be collected to remember your opt-out preferences.
